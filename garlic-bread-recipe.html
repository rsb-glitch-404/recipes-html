Garlic Bread!

About: Usually made with a French Baguette and something you pick up at the store, garlic bread is a nice snack or breakfast for one to have when in a savoury mood. This iteration in particular is a bit different, as it uses more accessible ingredients for you to make this whenever you want It will end up as a sandwich of which both slices of bread are covered with the garlic mix, and an egg will be added as garnish so you feel fancier and not as unhealthy.

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 egg
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 2 slices of ham
  • 15 grams of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of minced garlic
  • parsley as a garnish
  • honey as an optional garnish
Method:
  • Making the Spread
    1. Mix the butter with the garlic
    2. you can add parsley into the mix if you want
  • Preparing the Bread
    1. Add the ham and cheese slices on top of one slice of bread
    2. With the other piece of bread, make a slight concavity with a cup or whatever so that a divet forms
    3. Spread the butter spread across the top and sides of both slices of bread
  • Egg
    1. Crack and add the egg into the concaved part of the bread
    2. Sprinkle in some parsley if you want or some honey too
  • Bake (180ÂºC ~15 mins)

Congratulations, you just made yourself a piece of Garlic Bread
