Chocolate Chip Cookies!

About: A staple of a snack for those of all ages to enjoy, the chocolate chip cookie is one of the most famous cookies to have ever been made. Of course it started in America, however it's been around since the 1930s with multiple variations, whether its making the cookie itself chocolate, adding white chocolate, caramel, you name it. That being said, it means this recipe can only encapsulate the basic cookie, so if you're new to baking familiarise yourself with this recipe first before trying to customise it with your own toppings

Ingredients
  • 100 grams of Unsalted Butter
  • 80 grams of Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 160 grams of All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Chocoalte Bar/Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips
  • Pinch of Salt
Method:
  • Making the Batter
    1. Fold the Butter in with the Sugar in a bowl
    2. Add the lightly beaten egg in and keep folding
    3. Add the salt and vanilla in and fold again
    4. add the flour and fold once more until you're left with one entity
  • The Chocolate (bar)
    1. Dice it into small pieces (doesn't need to be even)
    2. Add it to the batter and mix it in till its evenly spread
  • The Chocolate (chips)
    1. Pour out some chocolate chips into your hand and sprinkle them across the batter
    2. Fold it into the batter till evenly distributed, if you think you want more then add and repeat
  • Baking
    1. Prepare a baking tray with a baking sheet greased a little so the cookies don't stick
    2. Ball up the batter and place them evenly across the tray like its a grid
    3. Bake it at 190Âºc for ~10-15 minutes, checking intermittently to see how consistent you want your cookies to be
    4. Take out once you're happy with the consistency you see (may be 10-15 mins, may not) and then eat when cooler

Congratulations, you just made yourself some Chocolate Chip Cookies
